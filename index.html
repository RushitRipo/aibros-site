FAQ

Common questions

Do I need any technical knowledge?
None at all. We handle everything — setup, deployment, and monitoring. You just tell us what you need automated and we build it.
How long does it take to get an agent running?
Most agents are live within 3–5 business days. More complex builds like trading bots take 1–2 weeks depending on the strategy.
What happens if something breaks?
We monitor all agents and fix issues as part of the monthly plan. You'll never be left debugging something on your own.
Are the trading bots connected to my real account?
We start with paper trading (simulated) so you can validate the strategy. Live account connection is your call once you're confident in the results.
Which AI model do the agents use?
We build with Claude (Anthropic), GPT-4, or local models depending on your privacy needs and budget. All options are available.
